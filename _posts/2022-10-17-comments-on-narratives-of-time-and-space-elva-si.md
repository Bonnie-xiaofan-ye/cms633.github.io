I found the section about translating dance movements into signs transcribed onto flatland pretty interesting. Tufts used several examples to help me reimagine how dance notation could be permanently perserved, something other than video recordings. Tufts also helped me realize the challenges beneath this visual-data translation, especially when we need to decompose many nearly universal yet invisible display techniques. It was eye-opening
